Child Safety Standards & CSAE Policy

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Ration Dukandar Manch (Mumbai) has a strict zero-tolerance policy against Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and the exploitation of children. We are committed to protecting minors and complying with all relevant global laws and regulations regarding child safety.

2. Reporting & Moderation

We do not allow content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates the sexual exploitation of children. Our moderation team actively monitors user-generated content.

  • Any user found sharing, posting, or promoting such content will have their account immediately terminated.
  • Content will be removed permanently from our servers.

3. Cooperation with Authorities

We will report any instances of CSAM to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and local law enforcement agencies in India, as required by law.

4. User Reporting Mechanism

Users can report inappropriate content or safety concerns directly within the app:

  • Use the "Report" function located on every post or user profile.
  • Contact our safety team directly at tydonincorp@gmail.com.
